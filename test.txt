Problem statement ->
In hospitals and clinical environments, critical patient context is often lost during shift changes, referrals, or transfers between care units, increasing the risk of delayed interventions, missed follow-ups, and medical errors.Although relevant information may exist across notes, observations, and treatment records, it is frequently fragmented, unstructured, or difficult to act upon during time-sensitive transitions.Build a secure solution that improves the safety and reliability of clinical handoffs by identifying and surfacing high-risk gaps, unresolved actions, abnormal trends, or critical changes in patient status that require immediate attention.The solution should support structured, explainable, and actionable handoff workflows rather than functioning as a simple record viewer or generic summarization tool.

    
    


System UI

    Page_1 : Login 
        - Username input field
        - Password input field
        - Login button

    page_2 : Dashboard
        - Total Wards
        - Total Patient
        - Total Doctors
        
    page_3 : Patient Details
        - After clicked on Ward
            - Patient list with search functionality
            - Filter options (e.g., by department, criticality)
            - Notification center for alerts and updates
        - After clicked on Patients
            - patient information card (as described above)
            - Timeline of recent events (e.g., medication changes, vital sign trends)
            - Actionable insights and recommendations (e.g., "Patient's blood pressure has been trending upwards, consider adjusting medication")

    page_4 : Handoff Workflow (cards displayed here)
            - Display patient information cards with color-coded status indicators
            - Highlight critical gaps or unresolved actions (e.g., "Missed dose alert for Metformin")
            - Provide structured handoff templates for clinicians to document and communicate key information during transitions
            - Create Card Button

            - Create Cards for each patient with the following information:
                - Doctor name
                - Doctor/nurse shift
                - Shift time
                - Shift date
                - Blood pressure
                - Heart rate
                - Temperature
                - Respiratory rate
                - Oxygen saturation
                - Medication name and information (current medication, next dose time, missed dose alert)
                - Sugar level
                - Color status (Red, Yellow, Green)

                -     # color status
                        - Red: Critical condition, immediate attention needed
                        - Yellow: Warning, monitor closely
                        - Green: Stable condition, continue regular monitoring

                        - Shift date: 2024-06-01
